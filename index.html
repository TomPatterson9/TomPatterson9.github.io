About Me



Profile

Hey, my name is Tom Patterson, I was born in Liverpool and I have been studying Computer Science with Software Development at University of Liverpool. I have recently finished third year and throughout my time I have worked with Java, Javascript and MySQL. For my dissertation I built a full stack application, it was a search engine for finding films on multiple platforms.

For my group project we created a timetable application for android which would allow students to sign into lectures from their phones when they were within a certain range of the lecturer theater. We also created the connection between our app and our database so we could allow users to login with a username and password.

I have always had a passion for computer science and web development in particular and this is where I have focused most of my personal projects such as creating a gaming news site. In my spare time I enjoy photography with a focus graffiti, I am currently building a web page to display these photos.
