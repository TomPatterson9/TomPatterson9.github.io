About Me



Profile

Hey, my name is Tom Patterson, I was born in Liverpool and I have studied Computer Science with Software Development at University of Liverpool. I have recently finished third year and throughout my time I have worked with Java, Javascript and MySQL. For my dissertation I built a full stack application, it was a search engine for finding films on multiple platforms.

Since finishing university I have been doing freelance work such as helping redesign a tutoring agency's website and I am currently working with a local garage to create them a new website.

I have always had a passion for computer science and web development in particular and this is where I have focused most of my personal projects such as creating a gaming news site. In my spare time I enjoy photography with a focus graffiti, I am currently building a web page to display these photos. I have attended two hackathons during my time at university, in the first we developed the front-end of a website which would help students find friends on campus. During my second hackathon our team managed to win first place we created an interactive video to help students gain employment by teaching them skills.
